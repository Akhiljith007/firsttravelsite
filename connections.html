CONNECTIONS


American Society of Travel Advisors External The Resources part of the web site includes a Member Directory but the home page also has a Find a Travel Advisor. Most of the research papers are not free. If you want to see what is import to the travel/tourism industry and what the members of this association are concerned about look at the Advocacy area for testimony and regulatory filings. Association of British Travel Agents External See the Industry Zone to understand the association and see what publications and reports the association publishes. Association of Canadian Travel Agents External Most of the information from the association is members only. Association of Retail Travel Agents External Most of the information from the association is members only. International Forum of Travel and Tourism Advocates External See the Links area for links to international associations and relevant government and nongovernmental bodies. Thomas Cook (archive) External The archive contains a vast wealth of information on travel history including extensive written records relating to the company’s history from the firm’s foundation in Leicester in 1841. They include unique letters, brochures, posters, passenger lists and guide books produced by the firm. There are also artefacts collected by the company. Travel Agent Central External This website has the latest news and trends in travel. It is the web presence for the magazine "Travel Agent." It is a resource for travel agents related to destinations and trends in travel as well as information for and about the industry. United States Tour Operators Association External Most of the information from the association is members only but there is information that is free that is geared to travelers and the Find a Travel Advisor tool may be the most helpful for those doing industry reserach. United Federation of Travel Agents' Associations External The UFTAA is the longest established negotiating partner with the leading travel and tourism organisations in the world and organizes Annual General Assembly; UFTAA FORUM on IATA Matters; UFTAA’s TOURISM FORUM, etc. information from the association is mostly for members only.
